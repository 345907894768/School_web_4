About PS Wawa-Cinna 780

PS Wawa-Cinna 780 is one of the best schools in Cinna Sparkle, Sparkly Wawas. Some humans and furries go there, but there is mostly water bottles going! We provide foods of all sections from humans, to water bottles. We also teach grades pre-k to 5th grades. So that means we have both a lower school and upper school. We also give of the best educations in Cinna Sparkle! If you like, you can apply!
